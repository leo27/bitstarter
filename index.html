4Snakes is a fun game based in the old Snakes game (yes, that one that you used to play hours in your first generation cell phone). But this time you will play with more people in the same board.

Play with your friends from different social networks (Facebook and Twitter) on a HTML5 platform, first clients launched will be latest generation browsers , iphone and android platforms. Users will fall in love with this game quckly.

FAQ

How will users obtain 4Snakes?
They will play this game from any browser with HTML5 support, and there will be android and iphone apps to play.
What is required from gamers to start playing 4Snakes?
They will be required to log in using his/her facebook, twitter or google+ account
